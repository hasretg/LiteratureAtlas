Project Overview

For the lecture "Multimedia Cartography" at ETH Zurich we got the task to create a web atlas with interactive maps based on literature texts. Particularly, the focus of the chosen literature texts was on novels. The chosen novels are: "Hausfrau" by Jill Alexander Essbaum, "Heidi" by Johanna Spyri , "The Night Manager" by John le Carre, "Hotel du Lac" by Anita Brookner, "Wolkenbruchs wunderliche Reise in die Arme einer Schickse" by Thomas Meyer and "The Fear Index" by Robert Harris.

The entire workflow for this project involved a deep analysis of the chosen literature texts with respect to mappable features, a decent visualization of the text analysis and additionally the creation of a web plattform, called web atlas, as an umbrella for all the resulting literary maps.

About us

Three member of the group, happily doing some mapping...
We are a group of master students enrolled at ETH Zurich. Thanks to the enrollment in the lecture "Multimedia Cartography" of the spring semester 2018, we got the rare opportunity to work in this project and to combine our passion for mapping, our enthusiasm for interesting and thrilling stories, and our skills of programming.

The work on this project during the semester was fun, but sometimes also very challenging. We faced numerous problems during our project. On the one hand, there were problems regarding the implementation of our atlas. On the other hand, it was also sometimes a bit complicated to extract all the geographical information from the chosen literature. Furthermore, since all the members in this group have different background in programming and mapping, it was sometimes also not easy to find fast a solution to a problem.

Nonetheless, we are proud of what we have achieved this semester and are happy to share with you the final product of our atlas.
